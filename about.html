What is designGNV?




designGNV is an initiative by the City of Gainesvilleâ€™s Neighbor-Centered Design team. We work on process improvement projects through the lens of human-centered design. Our goal is to improve how our neighbors experience city services and programs. What better way to do that than to invite our community into the design process!

We have many different kinds of activities for you to take part in. We do interviews, idea generation workshops, focus groups, surveys, usability testing, and many more. Your participation helps us figure out the best way to fix problems.

Tell us what you think!






How do we do this?







Understand






We conduct research to understand the needs of our community and the problems we face.

Some activities include:

  • Interviews and focus groups
  • Online surveys and digital tests
  • Design Thinking workshops





    • Design






    We explore ideas to solve problems. Our designers make prototypes based on these ideas.

    We design:

  • Digital and real-life experiences
  • Programs and services
  • Process improvements





    • Test






    We test these prototypes with our neighbors.

    Their feedback helps us ensure that our design:

  • Solves their problem
  • Solves it in the most efficient manner
  • Is fun and delightful to use!
